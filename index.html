Privacy & Data Policy
What CabLink accesses:
• Public wallet address only — never seed phrases or private keys
• Ride history stored locally on your device only
• Approximate location (Gaborone area only, for ride matching)
• Chat messages (end-to-end encrypted, not stored on any server)

What we do NOT do:
• Never sell your data or share with advertisers
• Never access funds beyond gas fee for claimReward()
• On-chain transactions are transparent and auditable
• No KYC required for testnet mode
